Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common queries about our programs

What are the eligibility criteria for admission?

Candidates must have completed 10th standard (SSC) with minimum 50% marks in Mathematics and Science. Age limit is 17-22 years at the time of admission.

Is there any entrance examination?

Yes, we conduct an entrance test covering Mathematics, Science, and General Reasoning. The test is designed to assess your aptitude for technical education.

What is the fee structure?

Annual fee ranges from ₹45,000 to ₹55,000 depending on the program. We offer scholarships and flexible payment options for deserving students.

What are the placement opportunities?

We have partnerships with 50+ companies including TCS, Infosys, Wipro, and many startups. Our average placement rate is 90%+ with packages ranging from ₹3-8 LPA.

Are there internship opportunities?

Yes, we provide mandatory internship programs in the final year. Students work with industry partners on real projects to gain practical experience.

What facilities are available on campus?

Modern computer labs, well-equipped libraries, Wi-Fi campus, cafeteria, sports facilities, and dedicated spaces for project work and innovation.
